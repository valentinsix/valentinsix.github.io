Hi there ! 👋🏼

My name is Valentin and I do research in Machine Learning.

I am currently a research intern in the Empirical Inference Lab at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems. I work with Prof. Bernhard Schölkopf and Lancelot Da Costa on using LLMs to help agents learn and plan effectively in the world they interact with.

In Fall 2026, I will begin a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Virginia, where I will be advised by Prof. Hadi Daneshmand. I was very fortunate to receive the UVA Provost Fellowship for my PhD.

Previously, I earned a Master’s degree in Computer Science from Georgia Tech, as well as Bachelor’s degree from Télécom SudParis, where I studied Mathematics and Physics.

Feel free to reach out!
