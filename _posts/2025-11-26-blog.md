Welcome to my own Latent Thoughts!

I never found a convincing reason to start a blog, but now I want a place to put down ideas as they come, without worrying about growing an audience or building a brand around it.

This is, first and foremost, a collection of ideas, thoughts, notes, and opinions. I share it here to centralize those ideas in one place and make them available to everyone, rather than leaving them scattered across private notes.

I call this blog **Latent Thoughts**, where I'll be writing as I think. Most of what ends up here starts as a half-formed idea I don't want to lose. I write it down and come back to it later, when it finally makes sense. Or maybe when I realize it didn't :)

I plan to share two kinds of posts here:

- The first is **research notes**: what I'm currently interested in, opinions I want to commit to instead of leaving vague, explanations I write mostly to organize my own understanding of a topic, and the occasional review of a paper I thought was worth digesting properly.

- The second is **meta-research**: not the research itself, but how I learn to do it, how to actually get research done instead of just thinking about doing it, how to find and talk to the right people... DISCLAIMER: I am in no pisition to give advice to anyone. If reading some of these notes sparks a useful thought for yourself, I will be very happy ; but this is no advice or guide to become a great researcher.

The reason I bother to write any of this down instead of just thinking it through is because thinking is messy: it's easy to lose a good idea, or rediscover the same one twice (and feel briefly proud both times), mostly because I never wrote the first one down. Putting it into words, especially on paper, is what makes an idea durable enough to build on instead of starting from scratch every time.
